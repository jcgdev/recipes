Ingredients:
 - One part long grain white rice
 - Two parts water
 - Salt to taste

Directions:
  1. Measure out the amount of rice you'd like to make and add it into a medium size pot.
  2. Measure out twice that amount of water and add it in.
  3. Add a couple of shakes of salt.
  4. Turn on the heat, cover and bring to a boil.
  5. When it boils, stir once, turn down the heat to a medium setting.
  6. Simmer for 10 to 15 minutes or until the rice is done, adding more water as needed.
